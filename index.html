Need help?

Activate/Deactivate Leap Motion:
Note: All commands are in context relative to the leap motion device, not the screen. Keep this in mind!
To activate, place your right hand, palm hovering above controller. To deactivate, close your right hand into a fist.

Move in Google StreetView:
To turn right, slowly tilt your right hand pinky-side down. To turn left, slowly tilt your right hand thumb-side down. To move forward, shift right hand forward and quiver from side-to-side.

Open/Close Sidebar:
Hover left hand palm down above controller. Verify that left hand has registered with Leap Motion; left hand button should turn green. To open, swipe to the right, left hand palm facing right. To close sidebar, swipe to the left, palm facing left.

Toggle Markers:
Use left hand for these commands:

  • Tweets: Use index finger and point to the screen.
  • Google Places: Use index and middle finger (peace sign) and point to the screen.
  • Bus Stops: Use thumb, index and middle finger and point to the screen.
  • Instagram: Tuck in thumb, only having the four fingers pointing to the screen.

Toggle StreetView Only/Side-by-Side:
To open, place right hand up and to the right of the leap motion (but not too far forward). Extend your index finger forward with the rest of your fingers tucked.
To close, same hand position as open, index and middle finger extended.
