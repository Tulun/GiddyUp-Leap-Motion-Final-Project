Activate/Deactivate Leap Motion: To activate, place open right hand palm above controller. Close palm into fist to deactivate.

Move in Google StreetView: To move right/left, place right hand palm perpendicular to controller, pointing to screen, and slowly tilt to the right/left. To move forward, place open right hand palm above controller and towards screen, and wiggle your fingers.

Open/Close Sidebar To open sidebar, place right hand palm perpendicular to controller, pointing to screen, and swipe palm from right to left. To close sidebar, rotate palm with thumb down, and swipe from left to right.

Toggle Markers Point index to screen for first checkbox; add middle finger to toggle second checkbox, etc.
